### Integers in GO

- `== equal to`
- `!= not equal to`
- `< less than`
- `<= less than or equal to`
- `> greater than`
- `>= greater than or equal to`

- `+ unary positive (no effect)`
- `- unary negation`

- `& bitwise AND`
- `| bitwise OR`
- `^ bitwise XOR`
- `&^ bit clear (AND NOT) << left shift`
- `>> right shift`
